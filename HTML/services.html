Teaching Philosophy

I believe that students of any age and starting ability can benefit from cello lessons. Each student experiences music in a different way and it is my job as a teacher to help each student reach his or her goals in a fun and engaging way. I focus on proper technique to enable tension free and enjoyable playing. Students may start at any age.

Studio Policies

Students are expected to practice regularly. Cancellations must be made with 24 hours advance notice or a late fee may be charged. Payments must be made in full before the start of each lesson.
